# TranspoLink – Digital Platform for Smart Goods Transportation

## Overview

The proposed solution is an on-demand truck booking platform that connects customers with nearby truck drivers for fast, reliable, and cost-effective goods transportation. Similar to ride-hailing models, users can instantly book trucks of various sizes, track their shipment in real time, and negotiate or accept transparent pricing.

## Key Features

- Real-time truck booking (mini trucks to large carriers)
- Live location tracking of drivers
- Fare estimation and optional bidding system (like inDrive)
- Driver and customer rating system
- In-app chat/call for coordination
- Secure digital payments and booking history

## Value Proposition

This platform solves inefficiencies in the traditional logistics sector by digitizing truck hiring, reducing dependency on middlemen, lowering costs, and improving transparency. It benefits both customers (quick, affordable transport) and drivers (more job opportunities and higher utilization).

## Target Users

- Small businesses & retailers
- Individuals shifting homes or goods
- Logistics and supply chain operators

## Business Model

- Commission per ride (5–15%)
- Premium driver subscriptions for higher visibility
- Surge pricing during peak demand
